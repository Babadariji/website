About Us

Rehoboth Childrens House is a co- educational day school in the Lekki Area of Lagos State,
offering crèche, pre- school, nursery and primary education for children from age 0-12 years.
Children can join the school throughout the academic year.
Rehoboth Children’s House prides itself in maintaining high academic standards,
encouraging every child to develop enthusiasm for learning, good manners
and self-discipline. We teach children to believe “that they can do more than they think”.
Our preschool program is filled with investigation, exploration, and discovery, where active
and enthusiastic children can refine and expand their skills and knowledge.Through intentional
teaching practices,
purposefully-designed learning environments, and enriched activities, we ensure
preschoolers have joyful, appropriate, and meaningful learning experiences that lead to success in school and in life.
Our child-centered approach ensures that children are exposed to a diverse array
of learning activities and that they
thrive in all areas of child development, creative thinking, complex problem solving, empathetic
collaboration, curious investigation, and astute decision making.
